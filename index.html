THE MISSION

Ripple is an interactive virtual environment which educates users on the threats facing coral reefs.

 

The LEAP Motion

The LEAP Motion device detects hand gestures, so you can wave your hands to control your fish and propel forward.

Explore a 3D World

Experience a fully rendered 3D environment built entirely in Unity. This includes texturing, lighting, and filtering.

Find the Facts

As you explore, you'll come across various facts about the threats facing oceans and coral reefs today. These include Overfishing, Marine Debris, Oil Pollution, Acidification, and Mass Extinction.
